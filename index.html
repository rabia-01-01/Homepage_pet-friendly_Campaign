About the Campaign 🐶🐱

The Pet-Friendly City Campaign is a grassroots initiative dedicated to transforming urban spaces into welcoming environments for pets and their humans. 🐾

Our mission is to advocate for more pet parks, promote responsible pet ownership, and foster a sense of community among animal lovers. From organizing clean-up drives to hosting adoption events, we believe every city can be a happier, healthier place for our furry friends. 🏙️

Whether you're a lifelong pet parent or simply an animal admirer, your passion and energy can help us make a difference. ✨
